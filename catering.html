Cater your event with Demo's Craveable Pizza!

Demo's Pizza is the perfect staple for any event, with dough made from scratch, an original sauce recipe, and three fresh signature cheeses. Each large pizza includes eight slices and is ideal for three adults. From birthdays to meetings, any event is sure to be delicious with pizza from Demo’s.

Sides, Salads & Desert

Bring even more to the table with sides, salads, and dessert from Demo’s! Enjoy crisp salads, fresh-baked CheezyBread, and a sweet finish like our delicious CinnaSquares. There’s no better way to complete your event and leave everyone satisfied.

Subs & Wings

Looking for more than pizza? Just look to our delicious subs and wings. From our Italiano and Chicken Club subs to meaty chicken wings with tangy BBQ or classic hot sauce, you’re guaranteed to please your crowd with these great additions to your event.

WE DELIVER

We know planning an event can be stressful, that’s why our professional and convenient delivery is here to make it easier. We’ll bring a great meal to you, so you can bring your best to every guest.

TIMING

Our dough is made from scratch in every store, every day. Therefore, orders must be placed at least 24-hours in advance. For events sooner than 24 hours, please call your local store for options. Cancellations must be made 4 hours prior to carryout or delivery.

QUESTIONS
